Frequently asked questions

The details, answered simply.

What is included?
Housing, all utilities, and washer and dryer. Rooms are fully furnished, a meal plan is available, WiFi is a plus, and we offer pharmacy delivery so medication comes straight to the home. It is an all-inclusive stay, one simple monthly payment.
Is there a deposit?
Yes. A non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a room.
Is proof of income required?
Yes. Proof of income is required as part of the application process.
When do I get the address?
After screening. Property locations are shared only after initial screening, to protect the privacy and safety of every resident.
Who can live here?
We welcome adults from many walks of life, including veterans, seniors, re-entry individuals, those living independently with mental health needs, and people who have experienced homelessness. What matters most is respect for the home and the people in it.
Do you have separate homes for men and women?
Yes. We keep separate homes for men and for women, so everyone can feel safe and comfortable. We also have a coed home, though space there is limited. When you reach out, we will help place you in the right home.
Is there a background check?
Yes. A background check is part of the application process, and it helps keep every resident safe. There is no judgment here. We are all moving forward and starting a new chapter.
Do I have to share a room?
No. We offer both private and shared rooms, so you can choose what fits you best, based on availability.
How do I apply?
Send us your basic information through the inquiry form, or call us. Someone will reach out to walk you through the next steps.
How much does it cost?
We share pricing and availability once your application has been submitted and reviewed. Start with the inquiry form, and we will walk you through the details from there.
