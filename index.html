How it works

A simple, respectful path to move-in.

A few clear steps protect every resident and help us place you in the right home.

1

Reach out

Send us your basic information through the website, or call. We answer questions about availability and pricing.

2

Apply

Complete a short application. Every applicant goes through a background check as part of the process.

3

Screening

We confirm proof of income and review your application. Placement depends on availability and screening.

4

Reserve & move in

A deposit reserves your room. The property address is shared once screening is complete, then you move in.

Start your inquiry

Important information

  • A non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a room.This holds your space while we finish the process.
  • Proof of income is required as part of the application.This helps make sure the home is a good, stable fit.
  • Property locations are shared only after initial screening.This protects the privacy and safety of every resident.
  • Contact us for availability, pricing, and move-in requirements.We are glad to walk you through the details.
